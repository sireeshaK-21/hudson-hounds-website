About Us

My name is Maria and I am the face behind Hudson Hounds. Hudson Hounds is a dog walking and pet sitting service, based in the Surrey Hills.

Caring for animals has always been my biggest passion in life. It’s not just a job for me, it’s my vocation. With years of hands-on experience—from kennels and catteries to veterinary practices—and studies at Merrist Wood College, I truly understand the unique needs of every pet.

I’ve also raised and cared for five dogs of different breeds, giving me practical, diverse handling experience. My personal dogs have included a Border Collie, a Yorkshire Terrier x Jack Russell, a German Shepherd, a Great Dane x Dobermann, and a Spaniel Mix. Each one taught me how every breed (and every dog) is different, requiring tailored attention and care.

I offer more than just a walk around the block—my goal is to provide enriching, fun, and safe adventures in the best walking spots around Milford. Whether you need a regular walking schedule or last-minute help, I’m flexible and happy to help.

I also offer additional services like nail clipping, bathing, brushing, and general grooming—at a fraction of vet prices—so your pet can stay comfortable, clean, and healthy.

When your pet is in my care, you can be assured they’ll be treated with the same compassion, patience, and dedication I give to my own animals.
